How's it going? I am a Full Stack Developer at Tower Insurance, with experience managing and leading projects in the IT and Building industry. My educational background begins with a Bachelors specializing in Environmental Science, leading to a Masters with First Class Honors in Information Technology. Allowing me to enjoy looking at the world from all lenses.

Information Technology and Software Development help fuel my passion for making a difference in the world while having fun and being at the forefront of what is happening! I believe Automation is the key to success and what I can help bring to the workplace. With mindless and mundane processes slowing down the workplace, people are continually getting lost and becoming disinterested with their purpose as an employee. Automating all that can be automated within the business, allows workers and the company to spend their energy and money on what matters. This can be succeeding as a business, NGO, or beginning as a startup. A fundamental tenet to this philosophy of thought is to create human-automation symbiosis, not replacing jobs but removing the non-human aspect of work, allowing people to focus on what people do best - being human.

My hunger to automate and create real worth within a business allows employees to do the human aspect of work. This has led me to successfully help pioneer the integration of the RPA SASS Ushur into Tower Insurance. This project, coupled with several small scale projects, has allowed Tower To free up hundreds of human hours so that the customer can be better focused on.

I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to bring my philosophy and skills to projects or workplaces. Email me here if you would like to talk more - jryland@protonmail.com .
